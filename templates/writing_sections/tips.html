Quick Writing Tips

Plan before drafting

Outline your thesis, 3 body ideas, and evidence points before you write.

Topic sentences first

Start each paragraph with a clear point that links back to the thesis.

Show evidence

Use data, quotations, or real examples; analyze how they prove your claim.

Prioritize clarity

Short sentences beat long, tangled ones. Remove filler words.

Transitions matter

Use signposts (for example, however, therefore) to guide the reader.

Revise in passes

Pass 1: ideas. Pass 2: structure. Pass 3: wording. Pass 4: grammar.
