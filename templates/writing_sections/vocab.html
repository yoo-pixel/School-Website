Vocabulary Boosters

Be precise

Replace generic verbs (make, do, get) with specific actions (craft, evaluate, achieve).

Watch nominalizations

Prefer verbs over noun forms: "analyze" instead of "conduct an analysis".

Academic register

Use formal but direct language; avoid slang and overly casual phrasing.

Hedge carefully

Use hedging (appears, suggests, may) when evidence is limited; avoid overclaiming.
