Strong vs. Weak Sentences

Weak

"People should recycle more because it is good."

  • Vague claim with no scope
  • No evidence or reasoning
  • No clear audience or urgency
Strong

"Expanding city-wide composting can divert up to 30% of landfill waste, cutting methane emissions and saving municipalities disposal costs."

  • Specific intervention + scale
  • Evidence (diversion %, cost angle)
  • Clear outcome (emissions + savings)
